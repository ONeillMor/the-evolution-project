How It Works

Large Scale Structured Conversations Creating Visions For The Future

Meetings

Facilitated meetings are held. Topics are arranged in advance online and the information from the meetings is collected.

Information

The collected information is input into a database containing the results of all other meetings. All the information is tagged and linked to other relevant information.

Presentation

The information is then presented online with all Visions, Benefits, Barriers, Solutions being connected and searchable

Discussion

Members discuss the information in our online forum adding and verifying information, to get to the heart of each topic.

Proposals

The flexible rubber of dreams, the Vision, meets the solid road of reality in the Proposals. Members build formal structured plans of action to tackle part of the set, large or small, using verified information which has come through the sets.

Why It Works

Buckminster Fuller’s insightful statement probably describes the aims of The Evolution Project most succinctly.

At this moment in the world, we have unprecedented resources available to us: tools for communication and collaboration, technologies and physical resources, inspirational people with incredible knowledge and skills. And enormous numbers of people with dreams of a better future.

The Evolution Project provides the tools and processes that enable us to bring all of these resources together to build a better system. It helps people create a set of visions for the future, it then breaks down the task of creating these visions into achievable projects, it then provides the tools to enable us take the actions required to bring about those visions.

The Project is a level playing field for ideas, where all voices are properly heard and all inputs go into the data sets. It encourages people to dream big, to aim straight up, for the stars. What is the best we can be? Why aim anywhere else? It allows for very large participation and is structured to bring all resources to bear on the tasks which emerge from the process.

From Visions To Proposals

Conversations are the building blocks of the Evolution Project. It is a large-scale structured conversation generating sets of Vision-Benefits-Barriers-Solutions-Proposals, presented visually on the site.

Vision: What do we want? For ourselves, our future, our children?

Benefits: How would this Vision affect us and our world?

Barriers: Why is this not our current reality?

Solutions: What can dissolve the Barriers?

Discussion: Takes place first in meetings, the website is secondary. Human interaction is an essential component to the project. The discussion continues in the forum, adding and verifying information, to get to the heart of each topic.

Clean Oceans

To date, The Evolution Project has facilitated 265 meetings taking place throughout the island of Ireland and has a total of 1536 members.
The vision of Clean Oceans has been discussed in 212 of those meetings ranking it in 3rd place.

We see a world where:

  • - The Ocean and seas are clean and healthy.
  • - Sea life can thrive as nature intended.
  • - People make use of the ocean and seas without harming them.
  • - There is no plastic waste, no oil spills, no dumping.

Benefits : 125

  • Prevent environmental breakdown / catastrophe
  • Protect sea life
  • Create green job opportunities

Barriers : 42

  • Worldwide problem and has many causes
  • Cost
  • Technical Challenges

Solutions : 136

  • The Ocean Clean Up Project
  • Stop input of plastics / Biodegradable plastics
  • Stop Bottom Trawling

  • Sign: Packaging reform
  • Support The Ocean Clean up Project
